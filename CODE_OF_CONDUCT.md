# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We as contributors and maintainers pledge to make participation in this project a
harassment-free experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability,
ethnicity, gender identity and expression, level of experience, nationality, personal
appearance, race, religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment:
- Using welcoming and inclusive language
- Being respectful of differing viewpoints and experiences
- Gracefully accepting constructive criticism
- Focusing on what is best for the community

Examples of unacceptable behavior:
- Harassment, insulting/derogatory comments, or personal attacks
- Publishing others' private information without consent
- Using this toolkit's synthetic-media capabilities to create or distribute
  non-consensual, deceptive, or harmful content outside of the documented
  research/testing scope of this repository

## Enforcement

Instances of unacceptable behavior may be reported to the repository maintainer. All
complaints will be reviewed and investigated.
